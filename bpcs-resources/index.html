BPCS Resource Allocation Calendar (RAC)

Resources

All the data, including the resource list, comes from Google Sheets. The instructions below are specific to the spreadsheet used.

Adding A New Resource

  1. Open the Google Sheet.
  2. Add a sheet.
  3. Refresh the RAC. The new resource will be listed in the Resources menu.

Editing An Existing Resource

  1. Open the Google Sheet.
  2. Rename the sheet.
  3. Refresh the RAC. Your updated dashboard will be listed in the Resources menu.
