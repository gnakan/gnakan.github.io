What do you really want to know about your business?

The real value from data comes from it's ability to provide insight that drive actions to directly impact your business. Traditional business intelligence solutions focus on looking at what happened in the past, hoping that insight can be pulled by a select few within an enterprise.

With a Blueprint Consulting Services applied analytics solution, your entire enterprise will be empowered to get answers to the questions that truly matter. You'll get insight into what will happen, why it will happen, who it will happen to and what should be done to progress your business forward.

How do you interact with your data?

Interaction is a key component to business intelligence. Standard dashboard solutions typically focus on data views that matter to a single individual or a select group and lack the personalized communication necessary to extract real value from your data.

At Blueprint Consulting Services, we believe that true insight comes from inquiry; the ability for you to build your own understanding quickly and easily from the information provided. Our applied analytics solutions manifest insight through a variety of approaches, from intuitive and compelling dashboards and scorecards to augmented (AR) and virtual reality (VR) applications that expose, enhance and enable next generation data interaction.

What does your data infrastructure look like?

All data driven solutions require a solid foundation in place that can ingest data at the speed required, store the data safely and expose the data in a manner that scales with enterprise demands. Scattered single instance relational databases and spreadsheets managed by a select number of individuals are a costly bottleneck to any enterprise.

Our applied analytics solutions focus on cloud computing and storage technologies so your initial infrastructure investment costs are low and accelerate the ability for your enterprise to take full advantage of all your data. From consulting your existing technology teams on cloud services migration to providing the professional service to execute your strategy, we can provide the knowledge and personnel necessary for implementing and managing a successful cloud data infrastructure.

Powered by our demonstrated expertise in data ingestion, cluster computing, machine learning, and data visualization, our solutions and products incorporate a variety of enterprise grade cloud technologies, platforms, libraries and tools.
