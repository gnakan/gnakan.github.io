Ryan Neal

President

Ryan Neal is a talented investor, director, and advisor with more than ten years of experience in collaborating with the owners and managers of private companies to increase their value. With a focus and specialty in high-tech and custom manufacturing environments, Ryan brings a diverse skill set and the passion to affect long-lasting, strategic partnerships with companies and individuals.

Ryan has significant experience leveraging resources in many industries to make companies successful, including his experience in business development, project management, change management, and strategic consulting. Ryan brings a dedicated commitment to constant improvement, which is critical to achieving transformative results.

Danielle Funston

Ian Hunt

Managing Director

Ian Hunt brings more than 20 years of experience as a customer support leader building global multi-tiered and multi-channel support solutions that optimize customer satisfaction. Ian is responsible for the management of our Customer Experience & Support Business and has a background in Service Management, Customer Experience Design, Technical support, Contact Center Solutions, and Operations.

Most recently Ian has led various divisions of Microsoft’s Global Customer Support Team with responsibilities for service availability and customer experience. He has both domestic and international management experience and a talent for making rapid assessments of diverse situational challenges, then developing and leading the resulting action plans. In his free time, Ian likes to travel, follows the English Premier League and in quieter times appreciates fine wine and a good book!
